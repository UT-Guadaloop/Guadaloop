Green Fund Texas 2024

We were announced as one of the winners of the Green Fund in spring 2024. We received a 26K grant from them during the Fall semester. This demonstrates reinforced support from UT Austin towards our project and more generally towards our efforts in sustainability. This money will assist our research for cleaner and more efficient transportation technology.
